Important Notes for Canadian Investors

Dividend Tax Credit

Most TSX dividends are "eligible dividends" and qualify for the enhanced dividend tax credit. This means most Canadians pay little to no tax on dividend income!

Capital Gains

Only 50% of capital gains are taxable in Canada. This makes capital gains more tax-efficient than regular income.

RRSP vs TFSA

RRSP: Tax deduction now, tax on withdrawal. Best if you're in a higher tax bracket now than in retirement.

TFSA: No tax deduction, but all growth and withdrawals are tax-free. Best for tax-free growth.

Disclaimer
